Things to do in Rio

Copacabana

Forte de Copacabana - great views and nice coffee shop

Cervantes - Great sandwichs! :) If you like pineapple, ask for a sandwich with it

The Beach - Just be bit careful if you decide to visit it in the evening

Ipanema

Beach at Posto 9 - If it is warm enough to enjoy the beach during the day, that's one nice place ot go

Beach at Arpoador - Great for watching the sunset

Toca do Vinicius - Bossa Nova store

Feira Hippie de Ipanema (artist's street market) - Every Sunday at General Osorio Square

Jardim Botanico

Botanical Garden - Rio's Botanical Garden is quite big. It can easily take half a day visiting

Parque Lage - If you have time and are in the area, might be worth checking it out

Botafogo

Casa da Matriz - Typical Carioca nightlife

Cobal Humaitá - Used to be my favorite Happy Hour place

Urca

Sugar Loaf - If you could do only one thing in Rio, that would be it :)

Laranjeiras

Christ Statue - And this would be thing number 2. Take the train up if possible.

City Center

Escadaria Selaron - Artsy steps, great place for pictures

Museu Historico Nacional - A bit of Brazilian history

Confeitaria Colombo - For afternoon tea

Rio Scenarium - Live music and nightlife
